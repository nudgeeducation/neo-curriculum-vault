Basic probability. Single and combined events. Tree diagrams, Venn diagrams, two-way tables. Mutually exclusive and independent events. Conditional probability (Higher). Probability as a fraction, decimal, or percentage.

- [[../../../03-overlays/edexcel-igcse/maths-a/ao3-handling-data|Edexcel IGCSE 4MA1 AO3]] (Handling data; 12–18% of grade) — within content area 6 (Statistics and probability)

- [[../../../03-overlays/edexcel-functional-skills/maths-l2/handling-information-and-data|Pearson Edexcel FS Maths L2 — Handling data]] *(partial — at the basic level: probability as fraction or decimal in everyday contexts)*

## Foundation vs Higher

Foundation: probability scale, single-event probability, combined events with tree diagrams, Venn diagrams.

Higher: conditional probability, probability without replacement, more complex Venn diagram problems.
