Data collection and sampling. Averages (mean, median, mode) and range. Charts and graphs (bar charts, pie charts, scatter graphs, frequency tables, histograms — Higher). Cumulative frequency, quartiles, interquartile range (Higher). Critical interpretation of statistics in real-world contexts.

## Tested by

- [[../../../03-overlays/edexcel-igcse/maths-a/ao3-handling-data|Edexcel IGCSE 4MA1 AO3]] (Handling data; 12–18% of grade) — within content area 6 (Statistics and probability)
- [[../../../03-overlays/aqa-gcse/maths/ao1-standard-techniques|AQA GCSE 8300 AO1]] applied to the Statistics content domain
- [[../../../03-overlays/edexcel-functional-skills/maths-l2/handling-information-and-data|Pearson Edexcel FS Maths L2 — Handling data]] (Ofqual 2018 §5) — calculate mean, range; interpret and represent data; use probability

## Foundation vs Higher

Foundation: averages from lists and frequency tables, simple charts and graphs, scatter graphs and correlation.

Higher: histograms with unequal class widths, cumulative frequency and quartiles, comparing distributions, sampling methods.
