# Refining through experimentation with media

Trying things. Selecting and experimenting with appropriate media, materials, techniques, and processes. Iterating — keeping what works, abandoning what doesn't, learning from both. The discipline of *making* as a way of thinking. Demonstrating range and judgement in material choice.

## Tested by

- [[../../../03-overlays/edexcel-igcse/art-design/ao2-refining-and-experimenting|Edexcel IGCSE Art and Design AO2]] — refine work by exploring ideas, selecting and experimenting with appropriate media, materials, techniques and processes (25%)
- [[../../../03-overlays/aqa-gcse/art-design/ao2-refining-and-experimenting|AQA GCSE Art and Design AO2]] — refine work by exploring ideas, selecting and experimenting with appropriate media, materials, techniques and processes (25%)

## Suggested evidence types

- Material experiments (e.g. *"three different ways of representing the same subject"*)
- Process annotation — what was tried, what worked, what didn't, what was learned
- Iteration sequences (early draft → refined version → final work)
- Photo logs of physical making process
