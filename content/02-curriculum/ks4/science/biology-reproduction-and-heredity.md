Sexual and asexual reproduction. The human reproductive system. Menstrual cycle and contraception (in coordination with [[../../../02-curriculum/statutory/rshe/sex-education/index|RSHE — Sex Education]]). Pregnancy, fetal development, and birth. Inheritance: alleles, genotypes, phenotypes, monohybrid crosses. Genetic variation and evolution. Genetic disorders.

Reproductive biology content overlaps with the [[../../../02-curriculum/statutory/rshe/sex-education/index|RSHE Sex Education]] strand. NEO delivers these through the same practitioner-led model, with care given to age-appropriate framing and parental opt-out arrangements (Sex Education only — Health Education and Relationships Education are not opt-out).
