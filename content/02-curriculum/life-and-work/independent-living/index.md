# Life and Work — Independent Living (canonical)

The practical skills of running one's own life. Housing, healthcare, personal admin, daily-life self-organisation, public services, transport, emergencies. Held as **canonical content in its own right** — for many EBSNA / SEMH learners, these are the genuinely hard skills, harder than English Literature, and they deserve the same explicit teaching.

## Strands

- [[housing-and-home|Housing and home]] — renting, sharing, tenancy, bills, home management
- [[healthcare-and-self-care|Healthcare navigation and self-care]] — registering with services, using them, advocating for oneself
- [[personal-admin|Personal admin]] — banking, ID, forms, utilities, contracts, the digital bureaucracy of being an adult
- [[daily-life-self-organisation|Daily-life self-organisation]] — cooking, planning, time management, transport, emergencies

## Cornerstone framing

[[../../../01-cornerstones/connection|Connection]] (talking to landlords, GPs, councils, banks, transport staff), [[../../../01-cornerstones/reflection|Reflection]] (knowing one's own needs, advocating for them), [[../../../01-cornerstones/movement|Movement]] (transport, navigation, embodied competence in unfamiliar environments), [[../../../01-cornerstones/nutrition|Nutrition]] (cooking and food).
