# KS3 Science — Forces, energy, and the universe

The physics content of KS3 Science. Energy and energy transfer. Motion and forces. Waves and sound. Electricity and magnetism. The particle model (shared with chemistry). Space physics — Earth, Sun, Moon, the Solar System.

## NC outcomes (paraphrased)

Pupils should be taught to:

- **Energy** — energy stores and transfers; conservation of energy; energy resources; efficiency
- **Motion and forces** — speed, distance, time; forces and their effects; balanced and unbalanced forces; gravity; pressure (in fluids and solids)
- **Waves** — properties of waves; sound; light; reflection and refraction
- **Electricity and electromagnetism** — current, voltage, resistance; series and parallel circuits; magnets and electromagnetism
- **Matter (particle model)** — see also Matter and chemistry strand; pressure, density, kinetic theory
- **Space physics** — the Solar System; Earth, Sun, Moon; seasons; gravity in the Universe
