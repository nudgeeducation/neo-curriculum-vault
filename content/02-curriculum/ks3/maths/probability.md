Probability scale. Single-event probability. Theoretical and experimental probability. Simple combined events.

## NC outcomes (paraphrased)

Pupils should be taught to:

- Record, describe, and analyse the frequency of outcomes of probability experiments using tables and frequency trees
- Apply ideas of randomness, fairness, and equally likely events
- Express probability as a fraction, decimal, or percentage
- Calculate probability of single events and of simple combined events
- Use Venn diagrams to record outcomes
