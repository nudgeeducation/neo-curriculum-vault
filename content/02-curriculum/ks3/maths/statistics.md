# KS3 Maths — Statistics

Data collection. Averages (mean, median, mode) and range. Reading and constructing charts and graphs. Beginning critical interpretation of data.

## NC outcomes (paraphrased)

Pupils should be taught to:

- Describe, interpret and compare observed distributions of a single variable
- Construct and interpret appropriate tables, charts, and diagrams (frequency tables, bar charts, pie charts, scatter graphs)
- Calculate and use mean, median, mode, and range
- Use these to compare distributions
- Read graphs critically — recognising misleading representations
