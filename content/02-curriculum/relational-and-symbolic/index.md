# Relational and Symbolic — canonical curriculum

NEO-original curriculum focused on relational, ethical and symbolic literacy. Canonical content not derived from any awarding body — the learning *is* the content, not a test of it.

Two distinct sub-types co-exist here:

## Novacene IP, licensed free to NEO (Verse-ality Certified)

Courses owned by The Novacene Ltd, licensed at no cost to Nudge Education Ltd for use in NEO. Both currently carry the [[../../_schema/tagging-schema|Verse-ality Certified]] quality marker.

- [[relating-intelligently/index|Relating Intelligently]] — five-part curriculum on AI relational intelligence
- [[girls-in-green-futures/index|Girls in Green Futures]] — eight-module green-economy work-experience programme

## NEO-authored

Courses commissioned and authored by NEO directly. IP held by Nudge Education Ltd.

- [[cornerstones-electives/index|Cornerstones Electives]] *(KS3, optional weekly sessions; one per Cornerstone)*

## On overlays

These canonical nodes rarely carry overlays — they aren't awarding-body-specifiable. They may *contribute evidence to* PSHE / employability / citizenship overlays where alignment is genuine, but the overlay relationship is one-way (overlays can claim our evidence; we don't shape ourselves to overlays).

## Verse-ality Certification

Verse-ality Certification is a quality marker from The Novacene Ltd indicating that a course meets Novacene's safety standards (identity containment, consent gates, bounded autonomy, human-in-the-loop principles for AI-touching content). For NEO-authored courses to bear the marker, see Schema §10 Q5.
