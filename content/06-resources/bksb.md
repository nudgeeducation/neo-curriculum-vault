# bksb — Basic and Key Skills Builder

The standard Functional Skills diagnostic and revision platform used in the existing Nudge service (per the FS coordinator's note, May 2026).

## What it provides

- Initial assessments
- Diagnostic assessments (skill-by-skill)
- Learning resources mapped to FS outcomes
- Tracking and reporting
- Alignment with multiple awarding bodies (Pearson, City & Guilds, Open Awards, Highfield, NCFE)

## Use in NEO

Referenced from [[../03-overlays/edexcel-functional-skills/index|Functional Skills]] outcome pages as the standard revision/diagnostic platform. Outcomes tagged `[[exam-pathway/pass-functional-skills]]` link to the relevant bksb units.

## Relationship to assessment

bksb is a *learning and revision tool*, not an awarding body. Learners sit FS exams via the awarding body their LA / commissioner has registered them with (typically through Pass Functional Skills). bksb supports preparation; it does not award qualifications.
