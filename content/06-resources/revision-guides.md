# Revision guides

Awarding-body-aligned revision guides sourced via Amazon or publisher direct, in line with whichever board a learner is sitting exams through.

## Sourcing pattern

For each Exam Pathway tag, the relevant publisher's revision-guide ISBNs and links are recorded on a per-spec basis on the outcome pages — for example:

- AQA GCSE → CGP, Oxford, Pearson revise-AQA guides
- Edexcel IGCSE → Pearson-published revise-Edexcel guides; Hodder
- Pearson Edexcel UK GCSE → Pearson revise-Edexcel
- Functional Skills → Pearson, Pass Functional Skills' own materials, bksb

Ordering and procurement sits with the Curriculum Lead (when filled) and the practitioner team. Costs are passed through to commissioners or families per the [NEO Admissions Policy](https://nudgeeducation.github.io/nudge-policy-vault/) and the Terms and Conditions.
