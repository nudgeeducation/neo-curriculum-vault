NEO is pursuing **Online Education Accreditation Scheme (OEAS)** accreditation only — not DfE registration as an independent school, not ISI inspection. This is the route to DfE recognition for online provision.

## The four anchor tags

- `[[oeas/curriculum-breadth]]` — coverage across the curriculum areas required by OEAS
- `[[oeas/curriculum-balance]]` — appropriate weighting and pacing across the offer
- `[[oeas/curriculum-rigour]]` — depth and demand of teaching, evidenced through outcomes and assessment
- `[[oeas/teaching-quality]]` — observed and evidenced quality of live and asynchronous delivery

## Use in the vault

Every node that helps NEO evidence an OEAS criterion should carry the relevant OEAS tag, so that a single tag query produces the audit pack on demand. The OEAS audit pages live on the **private** side of the hybrid vault (schema §8.2) and are not published to the public Quartz site.

## Anchor in T&L Policy

See [Teaching and Learning Policy v04.26](https://nudgeeducation.github.io/nudge-policy-vault/neo-only/neo-teaching-and-learning-policy) §1 — *"This policy aligns with OEAS accreditation criteria relating to curriculum and teaching quality."*
