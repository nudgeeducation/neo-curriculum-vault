Four PfA outcomes from the [SEND Code of Practice for England](https://www.gov.uk/government/publications/send-code-of-practice-0-to-25). PfA is the spine of ASDAN's My Independence and Preparing for Adulthood programmes, and a common framing for EHCP outcome statements.

## The four outcomes

- `[[pfa/employment]]` — paid employment, including self-employment
- `[[pfa/independent-living]]` — having choice and control over one's life and good housing
- `[[pfa/friends-relationships-community]]` — community participation
- `[[pfa/good-health]]` — physical and mental health

## Use in NEO

The PfA tag is auxiliary — applied where a curriculum outcome has clear PfA alignment. Particularly useful for **EHCP outcome mapping** in commissioner reports: where a young person's EHCP names PfA outcomes, the vault can evidence those directly from curriculum delivery via a tag query.

## Care note for EBSNA cohort

Preparing-for-adulthood language can land oddly with younger or more anxious learners. Possible alternative: tag as PfA in back-end metadata but use softer language in learner-facing pages. See schema §10 Q5.
