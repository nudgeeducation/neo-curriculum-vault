Original expression, making, play, and divergent thinking. Learning where there is no single right answer, and the process of creation is itself educationally valid.

## Primary qualifications and strands

- [[../03-overlays/edexcel-igcse/art-design/index|Edexcel IGCSE Art and Design]] — fully portfolio-assessed
- [[../02-curriculum/relational-and-symbolic/girls-in-green-futures/index|Girls in Green Futures]] *(Verse-ality Certified)*
- [[../02-curriculum/ks3/creative-arts/index|KS3 Creative Arts]]

## Where Creativity appears as secondary

- *Relating Intelligently* — symbolic literacy, creative engagement with prompting
- ASDAN Personal Development Programme — many creative challenge options
- English Literature — interpretive and analytical creativity

## Statutory anchor

RSHE 2026 General Wellbeing (self-esteem, resilience, identity, self-worth) — the 2026 guidance explicitly names *"resilience, self-worth, integrity, courage"* as outcomes of effective RSHE; Creativity sessions evidencing this language map directly.
