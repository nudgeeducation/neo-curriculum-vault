# Edexcel IGCSE English Language A — Specification 4EA1

The threshold qualification commissioners, Ofsted, and post-16 providers look for first. Onscreen assessment available from 2023. Modular assessment not available for this subject — but session preparation can be paced and low-stakes until formal entry.

## Components

- **Component 1 (4EA1/01)** — Non-fiction Texts and Transactional Writing. Externally assessed. 2h 15min, 90 marks, 60% of grade. Section A: Reading (45 marks, AOs 1, 2, 3). Section B: Transactional Writing (45 marks, AOs 4, 5).
- **Component 2 (4EA1/02)** — Poetry and Prose Texts and Imaginative Writing. Externally assessed. 1h 30min, 60 marks, 40% of grade. Section A: Reading on poetry/prose (30 marks). Section B: Imaginative Writing (30 marks).
- **Component 3 (4EA1/03)** — Same content as Component 2 but non-examined assessment. 60 marks, 40% of grade. Alternative to Component 2.
- **Spoken Language Endorsement (4EA1/E)** — Optional. Separately reported (Not Classified / Pass / Merit / Distinction). Maximum 10-minute oral presentation.

Students take **Component 1 plus either Component 2 or Component 3**, plus optionally the Spoken Language Endorsement.

## Assessment Objectives

The same five AOs apply across Components 1, 2, and 3. The spoken language endorsement is separately graded.

- [[ao1|AO1]] — read and understand a variety of texts, selecting and interpreting information, ideas and perspectives
- [[ao2|AO2]] — understand and analyse how writers use linguistic and structural devices to achieve their effects
- [[ao3|AO3]] — explore links and connections between writers' ideas and perspectives, as well as how these are conveyed
- [[ao4|AO4]] — communicate effectively and imaginatively, adapting form, tone and register of writing for specific purposes and audiences
- [[ao5|AO5]] — write clearly, using a range of vocabulary and sentence structures, with appropriate paragraphing and accurate spelling, grammar and punctuation
- [[spoken-language-endorsement|Spoken Language Endorsement]] (4EA1/E)

## Anthology — Part 1 (Non-fiction; for Component 1 Section A)

Selections from: *The Danger of a Single Story* (Adichie); *A Passage to Africa* (Alagiah); *The Explorer's Daughter* (Herbert); "Explorers, or boys messing about?" (Morris); *127 Hours* (Ralston); "Young and Dyslexic? You've got it going on" (Zephaniah); *A Game of Polo with a Headless Goat* (Levine); *Beyond the Sky and Earth* (Zeppa); *H is for Hawk* (Macdonald); *Chinese Cinderella* (Yen Mah).

## Anthology — Part 2 (Poetry and Prose; for Components 2/3 Section A)

*Disabled* (Owen); "Out, Out–" (Frost); *An Unknown Girl* (Alvi); *The Bright Lights of Sarajevo* (Harrison); *Still I Rise* (Angelou); *The Story of an Hour* (Chopin); *The Necklace* (Maupassant); *Significant Cigarettes* from *The Road Home* (Tremain); *Whistle and I'll Come to You* from *The Woman in Black* (Hill); *Night* (Munro).
