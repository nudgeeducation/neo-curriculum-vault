# Edexcel IGCSE 4EA1 — AO1

> **AO1** — read and understand a variety of texts, selecting and interpreting information, ideas and perspectives.

*(Verbatim spec text private side only — © Pearson Education Limited 2025)*

## Tests canonical

- [[../../../02-curriculum/ks4/english/comprehension|KS4 English — Comprehension and inference of unseen texts]]

## Where assessed

Component 1 Section A. A mixture of short- and long-answer questions related to a non-fiction text from Part 1 of the Anthology and one previously unseen extract.

## Cross-overlay equivalents

- [[../../aqa-gcse/english-language/ao1|AQA GCSE 8700 AO1]] (close equivalent — also covers synthesis across two texts, which Edexcel's AO1 doesn't)
- [[../../edexcel-functional-skills/english-l2/reading|FS English L2 Reading]] (functional-comprehension equivalent)

## Awarding-body notes

Tested through both the anthology text (with which students will have prepared) and one previously unseen extract. Onscreen assessment is available for this component from 2023.
