# Edexcel IGCSE 4EA1/E — Spoken Language Endorsement (optional)

> **Overview** — the spoken language presentation may take a variety of forms, including a speech or talk by a student followed by questions; or a formal debate or dialogue (e.g. interview) with prepared extended responses to shared prompts followed by questions.

## Operational note for NEO

This endorsement is *optional*. A NEO learner can pass the IGCSE without it. For learners with EBSNA / SEMH backgrounds, the endorsement should be entered only when the learner is genuinely ready — and the assessment can take the form of a small-group dialogue or interview rather than a formal speech. See the canonical care note in [[../../../02-curriculum/ks4/english/spoken-english|Spoken English]].
