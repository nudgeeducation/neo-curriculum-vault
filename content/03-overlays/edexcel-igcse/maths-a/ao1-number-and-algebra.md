# Edexcel IGCSE 4MA1 — AO1 Number and Algebra

> **AO1** — Demonstrate knowledge, understanding and skills in number and algebra: numbers and the number system; calculations; solving numerical problems; equations, formulae and identities; sequences, functions and graphs.

## Awarding-body notes

Edexcel's AO structure is unusual in that the AO names directly identify content domains (Number/Algebra, Shape/Space/Measures, Handling data). AQA's AOs by contrast are skill dimensions (Use/Apply, Reason, Solve) cutting across all content. This means the same canonical KS4 Maths node typically connects to *one* Edexcel AO but *multiple* AQA AOs.
