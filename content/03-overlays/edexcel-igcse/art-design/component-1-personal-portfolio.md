# Component 1: Personal Portfolio

Internally-set personal portfolio of work based on centre-devised theme(s), project(s) or task(s). 72 marks. 50% of grade. Internally assessed, externally moderated.

The 120 guided learning hours for the qualification are distributed roughly 50% to this component. The portfolio shows the development of ideas and progress through the work, evidencing the four [[../../../02-curriculum/ks4/creative-arts/index|canonical AOs]].

## Submission requirements

- Three sheets of supporting studies (maximum size A2 per sheet)
- One sheet of final outcome(s) (maximum size A2)
- Work larger than A2 or 3D pieces are photographed (four photos per piece, A4 size, mounted on one A2 sheet)
- Coursework authentication sheet (Appendix 2 of the spec)

## NEO operational note

For online delivery, the portfolio is straightforward — supporting studies and final outcomes can be created and submitted digitally. Practitioner-led centre-devised themes are designed in dialogue with each learner, drawing on their interests and Discovery Phase profile. The 50% weighting on a portfolio that the learner has agency over is one of the strongest pedagogical fits for the EBSNA cohort in the Year 1 offer.
