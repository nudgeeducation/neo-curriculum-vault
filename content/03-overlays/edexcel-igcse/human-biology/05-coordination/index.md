## Therapeutic resonance

This is the content area most directly relevant to learners managing SEMH difficulties. Learning *why their nervous system does what it does* — fight/flight/freeze, hormonal cycles, sensory regulation — provides a structured framework for understanding their own experience. Per the Curriculum Rationale v2.0: *"taught well, with a practitioner who connects the science to the young person's lived reality, Human Biology is one of the most powerful subjects Nudge Access can offer."*
