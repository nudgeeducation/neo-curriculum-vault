# AQA GCSE 8700 — AO4

> **AO4** — Evaluate texts critically and support this with appropriate textual references.

*(Verbatim spec text private side only — © AQA)*

## Tests canonical

- [[../../../02-curriculum/ks4/english/critical-evaluation|KS4 English — Critical evaluation]]

## Where assessed

Paper 1 Section A only. Typically the final, longest question on the paper — asking students to evaluate the writer's success against a critical proposition.

## Cross-overlay equivalents

- [[../../edexcel-igcse/english-language-a/ao2|Edexcel IGCSE 4EA1 AO2]] *(partial — Edexcel weaves critical evaluation into AO2 rather than separating it)*

*Not present at FS Level 2.*

## Awarding-body notes

This AO is what most distinguishes AQA from Edexcel pedagogically — AQA explicitly asks for evaluative judgement, treating the reader as critic rather than purely as analyst.
