## Where assessed

Both papers, Section B. AO6 is dedicated to SPaG and accounts for 16 of the 80 marks on each paper — i.e. 20% of the writing mark.

## Awarding-body notes

AQA giving SPaG its own dedicated AO with explicit mark allocation makes proofreading and editing skills disproportionately important on AQA compared to Edexcel.
