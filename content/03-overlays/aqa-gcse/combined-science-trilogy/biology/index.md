## Cross-overlay

The biology subset closest in scope to [[../../../edexcel-igcse/human-biology/index|Edexcel IGCSE Human Biology]], but with significant differences:

- Includes ecology (Edexcel HB does not)
- Lighter on coordination and homeostasis depth than Edexcel HB
- Lighter on bones/muscles/joints than Edexcel HB
- Sits alongside chemistry and physics components, so total biology study time is roughly half what a learner on Human Biology gets
