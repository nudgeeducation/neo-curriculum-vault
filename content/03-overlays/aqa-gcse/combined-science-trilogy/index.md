# AQA GCSE Combined Science: Trilogy — Specification 8464

The dominant GCSE Combined Science pathway for the existing Nudge North East cohort. Counts as **two GCSEs** (double award). NEO supports learners registered with AQA via revision and prep alongside their primary NEO programme.

There is no AQA equivalent of Edexcel's Human Biology specifically — AQA offers single sciences (Biology 8461, Chemistry 8462, Physics 8463) or Combined Science Trilogy (8464) / Synergy (8465). For learners on the AQA pathway who would otherwise have done Edexcel Human Biology, the Trilogy biology component is the closest available match — but with a wider scope (because it sits alongside Chemistry and Physics) and less depth on body-system content.

## Papers

Six papers (two each in biology, chemistry, physics), each 1h 15min, 70 marks, 16.7% of grade. Foundation tier (5–1) and Higher tier (9–4) available.

## Working Scientifically

AQA gives Working Scientifically explicit billing — it accounts for at least 15% of overall qualification marks across all three sciences and underpins all content. See the canonical [[../../../02-curriculum/ks4/science/working-scientifically|Working scientifically]] strand for full discussion.

## Three components

- [[biology/index|Biology]] — cell biology, organisation, infection and response, bioenergetics, homeostasis and response, inheritance and variation, ecology
- [[chemistry/index|Chemistry]] — atomic structure, bonding, quantitative chemistry, chemical changes, energy, rate of reaction, organic chemistry, chemical analysis, atmosphere, using resources
- [[physics/index|Physics]] — energy, electricity, particle model, atomic structure, forces, waves, magnetism
