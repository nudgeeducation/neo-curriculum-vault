# ASDAN — Qualifications

ASDAN's regulated qualifications. Approved and regulated in England (Ofqual), Wales (Qualifications Wales), and Northern Ireland (CCEA). Portfolio-based, externally moderated by ASDAN.

## Treated as Overlay because

ASDAN is an awarding body (Ofqual-regulated). The portfolio-based assessment model differs from exam-based GCSE / IGCSE, but the relationship to canonical curriculum is the same: ASDAN's outcomes test canonical [[../../02-curriculum/life-and-work/index|Life and Work]] (and sometimes other canonical) content. Cross-link from each ASDAN qualification's outcome pages to the canonical content they evidence.
